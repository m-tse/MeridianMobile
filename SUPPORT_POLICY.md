# Support

**Time Zones**

Thank you for using Time Zones! We're committed to making sure you have the best experience possible with the app.

## Getting Help

If you're experiencing any issues, have feature requests, or just want to share feedback, we'd love to hear from you. We take every piece of feedback seriously and use it to improve the app for everyone.

## Common Tips

- **App not showing your local timezone?** Make sure Location Services are enabled for the app, or try restarting the app.
- **Times look incorrect?** Check that your device's date and time settings are set to automatic.
- **Want to reorder your timezones?** Long-press and drag to rearrange them.
